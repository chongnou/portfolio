About me

My love for fishing stems from my dad. I love it and fish whenever I can. I'm an active guy, hard working, and self driven. I recently quit my full-time job to become a software developer at devCodeCamp. I can build my own computers, mostly gaming because I love PC gaming. I am hoping to graduate from devCodeCamp and use my skills in the professional world. I served in the Army National guard for 6 years as a 19Delta, Calvary Scout. I also like to play volleyball and I am a big family man. I love family events.
